About the talk

Given in the frame of the Academic Unit of Physics' weekly seminars at the Autonomous University of Zacatecas - I had the pleasure of talking to friends and colleagues about a theory often relegated to pure mathematicians and computer scientists.

I argue then that there's much ado about such theories and their physical implications on AI, nonlinear system analysis and even deepening the nature of computation in the abstract sense.

The talk is given in Spanish but I think YouTube's auto-generated subtitles do a good job keeping up with my often relentless pace.
